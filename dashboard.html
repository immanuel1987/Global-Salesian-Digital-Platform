Pastoral Works

Each work: name, location, programs & activities, related resources — browse as cards or on the interactive map (7.2). Map markers open a summary, links to related content, and the full profile.

How to read these cards. Location is the geographic anchor; programs summarise what happens on site; related resources counts documents in GSDP linked to this work (prototype counts). Open a card for the full profile, or use Map and click any point for the same depth.
Filters apply to both list and map.
7.2 Map integration Interactive map — click a marker for summary, related content shortcuts, and full profile (same modal as the cards). Coordinates: prototype · CristO–Religio–style data.

Basemap © OpenStreetMap contributors · Marker popups are part of GSDP Pastoral Works.

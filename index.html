Intelligence Layer

AI-Powered Access to
Salesian Knowledge

Built on Databricks with Azure OpenAI, the platform transforms how the Congregation discovers, understands, and shares its knowledge.

🔍
Semantic Search
Natural language queries across the entire corpus. Ask "Drug addiction resources in South Asia" and get meaningful, contextual results.
📝
Document Summarization
Auto-generate summaries for full documents, specific sections, or custom prompts. Cut research time dramatically.
🌐
Multilingual Translation
Instant translation across EN, IT, ESP, FRA, POR — making every resource accessible to the global Salesian Family.
💬
Chat With Documents
Interactively query document content. Ask follow-up questions, get clarifications, and extract insights in a conversation.
✨
Smart Recommendations
Vector-embedding-based suggestions surface related resources you didn't know to search for, broadening discovery.
📊
Analytics Dashboards
Power BI / Looker dashboards tracking publications, event participation, geographic spread, and AI usage metrics.
